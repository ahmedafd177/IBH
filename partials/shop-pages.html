⚠ Returns Policy — Cosmetics & Beauty Products

Due to the hygienic and perishable nature of cosmetics, fragrances, and beauty products, we do not accept returns or exchanges once a product has left our store or been delivered, except within 24 hours of purchase, and only when the item is unopened, unused, in its original sealed packaging, and accompanied by proof of purchase (receipt or order ID). This policy protects the safety and authenticity of every product we sell.

1. Returns & Refunds

  • Returns are accepted only within 24 hours of the purchase or delivery time shown on your receipt/order.
  • The product must be unopened, unused, and in its original sealed packaging — for hygiene reasons, opened or used cosmetics, perfumes, and body care products cannot be returned under any circumstance.
  • A valid receipt, order ID, or proof of purchase is required for every return.
  • Items damaged, wrong, or defective on arrival are eligible for a replacement or refund regardless of the 24-hour window — contact us immediately with photos of the issue.
  • Refunds, once approved, are issued via the original payment method (M-PESA, card) or as store credit, within 3–5 business days.

2. Orders & Payment

  • We accept M-PESA, card payments, and cash/pay-on-delivery at select branches.
  • Orders are confirmed once payment is received or, for pay-on-delivery, once the order is placed.
  • Prices are listed in Kenyan Shillings (KES) and may change without prior notice; the price at checkout is the price charged.

3. Delivery

  • Delivery fees and timelines vary by zone and are shown at checkout before payment.
  • Please inspect your order on arrival — report any damage or discrepancy to us immediately.

4. Product Authenticity

  • All products sold by Inspiring Beauty Hub are 100% genuine and sourced from authorised suppliers.
